# 🔐 Privacy Policy

**Last Update:** 29.03.2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **Sticky Frog 🐸** handles data when the bot is used in Discord servers.

## ✨ What We Collect

Sticky Frog only collects and stores the minimum data required for its core features:

- Server IDs.
- Channel IDs.
- Sticky message content.
- Sticky message IDs.

## 💾 How We Use Data

The stored data is used only to:

- Keep sticky messages active in the correct channel.
- Restore sticky messages after a bot restart.
- Remove or update existing sticky messages when requested.

## 🔒 How Data Is Stored

- Data is stored locally in an SQLite database.
- The bot does not intentionally collect personal information such as real names, email addresses, or passwords.
- The bot does not sell, share, or monetize stored data.

## 👥 Data Access

Only the bot developer may access the stored bot data, and only for maintenance, debugging, or bot operation purposes.

## 🧹 Data Retention and Removal

- Sticky data remains stored as long as the sticky message is active.
- When a sticky is stopped, its related stored data is removed from the database.
- If the bot is removed from a server or data must be cleared, the relevant stored entries can be deleted manually.

## 🌍 Third-Party Services

Sticky Frog runs on Discord and therefore follows Discord’s own privacy practices and policies. Discord may separately process information according to its own terms and privacy policy.

## 🔁 Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time without any additional announcement. Any changes will be posted in this file, and continued use of the bot means you accept the updated policy.
